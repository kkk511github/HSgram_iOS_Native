# HSgram Screenshot Shot List

Capture final App Store screenshots after UI parity review and a real HSgram review account are available.

Required iPhone 6.9-inch set:

1. Email sign-in screen with HSgram identity.
2. Chats tab showing private chats, groups, folders, pinned and unread states.
3. Private chat thread with text, reply, media, and link preview bubbles.
4. Shared media browser showing media/files/links/GIF/voice/music tabs.
5. Group or channel management screen with members/subscribers and admin tools.
6. Settings screen showing account, privacy, notifications, storage, devices, and app lock.

Required iPad set:

1. Chats tab and selected conversation.
2. Group/channel management.
3. Settings and privacy.

Capture rules:

- Use only HSgram test data and HSgram accounts.
- Do not show Telegram names, assets, packages, screenshots, or source-derived UI artifacts.
- Do not show personal email addresses, phone numbers, device identifiers, or private production messages.
- Keep voice/video/live-streaming surfaces out of screenshots until those deferred features are migrated.
